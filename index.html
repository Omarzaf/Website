Where infrastructure, security, and regional politics collide.

Regional analysis matters because technology competition is not abstract. It moves through ports, cables, defense modernization, data rules, and alliance politics.

Institutions fail before they admit they are failing.

The recurring question in my work is how governance bodies process technical uncertainty, capture, legitimacy, and coordination failure before crisis makes the weakness obvious.

  • Organizational incentives in technical standard-setting
