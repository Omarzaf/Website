01

RDL Evidence Map.

The project maps public-record relationships around lobbying, institutions, and policy movement in the U.S.-China semiconductor and AI policy space. Its active surface is the V3_Epistemic pipeline and static map.

03

Release checks.

Built as an evidence-weighted map, not a rhetorical diagram.

The local release certificate records a passing public preflight, 7 passing tests, strict pipeline validation, resolved frontend manifest paths, and an active map that loads from a local HTTP server.

The public-use caveat is explicit: the map is exploratory and correlational. It is not a causal claim, not a claim about intent or wrongdoing, and not evidence that missing relationships do not exist.
