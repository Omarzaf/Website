03

Project Salam.

Education as logic-building, self-expression, and institutional trust.

Project Salam page

Project Salam is a research-based, skill-based curriculum for children in partner organizations. It combines first exposure to computers, chess as analytical practice, social-emotional learning, public speaking, creative technology, and mentorship.

The point was not simply to add activities. The point was to make education feel trustworthy in places where formal systems often arrive as discipline before care.

  • Partnered with welfare and community organizations.
  • Expanded from a foundation year into multi-site implementation.
  • Centered self-expression, mentorship, and practical digital literacy.

05

Davis Projects for Peace.

Theatre and dialogue as a way to rehearse coexistence.

The Davis Projects for Peace grant supports a three-month intervention in Lahore with Pashtun and Punjabi youth. The project uses role reversal, theatre, and facilitated dialogue to build a replicable peacebuilding model.

Deliverables include a public performance, a video documentary, and a guide for schools and community organizations that want to adapt the model.
